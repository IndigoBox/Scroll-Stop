Help Getting Started

To get started with Scroll Stop, open the options and enter a URL that
you would like Scroll Stop to be active on. You do not need to enter an
exact URL in these fields, Scroll Stop will check to see if the URL
contains the string you entered. For instance, typing "facebook" in the
URL field will match "www.facebook.com", as well as other URLs that
contain "facebook" in them.

Then, after you have chosen a URL or a match parameter, type in a value,
in pixels, for how far you want Scroll Stop to let you scroll before
stopping you.

Finally, you can specify the behavior Scroll Stop should take when you
reach your scroll limit. You can choose between closing the tab, removing
the contents of the tab, or redirecting to a different url.
